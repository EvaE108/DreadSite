Welcome to my little archive
Of those who didn't leave alive.
The riddles here are not just games—
They’re echoes left from screaming names.

I've buried clues in hidden sites,
In source code scraps and sounds unsight.
If you want to find the rest…
Think like me. Be my guest.

So to begin your quest and pass my trials like no one else,
You must first find the darkness in yourself.
